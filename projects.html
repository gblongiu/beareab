The Longtooth Collective was a spontaneous rupture in the Bay Area underground. Jon Howell (guitar), Dan Sneddon (drums), and Matt O’Brien (bass) enlisted vocalists Paul Isham, Harvey Rañola, and Gabriel Long for a single explosive document of noise/math/post-hardcore.

Recorded in 2008 and shelved for over a decade, it isn’t a comeback but a transmission of jagged time signatures, broken narratives, and the slow collapse of identity under grinding sludge riffs.

Howell’s angular phrasing carves out space, O’Brien’s low end bulldozes through the mix, and Sneddon’s drumming is always searching—turning every song into an unrepeatable battleground of intention. Members would later form or play in Kowloon Walled City, Tigon, Early Graves, Less Art, Ex Everything, and beareab.

Release date: April 4, 2008

  • Guitar: Jon Howell
  • Drums: Dan Sneddon
  • Bass: Matt O’Brien
  • Vocals: Paul Isham, Harvey Rañola, Gabriel Long
